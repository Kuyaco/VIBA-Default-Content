Conversational Assistant Valerie

VIBA Virtual Assistants, like Valerie, are conversational video bots using the latest natural language processing and AI technology to answer questions at near-human levels. Just engage with her in your normal voice, just like you are speaking with an actual human.

Valerie Standard Commands

Here are a few standard commands for Valerie:

  • Hey Valerie to re-engage with Valerie
  • Hold On or Pause to pause Valerie say Hey Valerie to begin again

  • Live Agent to transfer to an agent, if one is available
